Hello! I'm Wonuola

I'm a Lagos-based software engineer with a diverse background in accounting. After obtaining a major in accounting, I discovered my true passion for technology and made a successful transition into the world of software engineering.

Driven by my love for accessible web development, I strive to create websites that cater to the needs of all users, ensuring inclusivity and usability for everyone. I believe that technology should break barriers and empower individuals from all walks of life.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I find solace in listenig to BTS and exploring K-drama. These hobbies not only provide me with inspiration but also remind me of the importance of storytelling and the power of cultural exchange.

With a strong foundation in accounting and an unwavering dedication to the tech field, I am constantly seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and skills, while making a positive impact through my work. I am excited to contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of software engineering and strive towards a more inclusive digital future.
